HTML IFrame Element

The inline frame or <iframe> element enables designers to embed one webpage into another. In order to sucessfully setup an iframe element we need to set at least two attriutes, those are the src and title attributes. Here are our two examples today... an embedded youtube tutorial as well as an embedded recipe for chicken pastelles... yum!




When you look at the above iframes you will notice that the youtube content fits better onto the page, while the recipe does not look easily readable. If we look aat he settings I used in bpth iframes you will notice that in addititon to the minimal attributes of src and title I also set the width, height, frameborder and allowfullscreen attributes for the youtube video. This is why it appears as it does. When using iframes ensure that you take the time to adjust its presentation so that users find the content easily accessible.
